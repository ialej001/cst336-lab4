    • Welcome to Mercury!


    Mercury is the smallest planet of our solar system. It's mass is roughly 3.3 million trillion kilograms. The surface gravity is about one third of Earth. If you were to stand on the surface (and not melt!), you would weigh about one third of what you weigh on Earth! Here's an interesting fact about Mercury: it's solar day is twice as long as it solar year! In other words, all year round is your birthday~!
