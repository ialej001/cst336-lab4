    • Welcome to Venus!

    Venus is the solar system's second planet. Its day is longer than its year. However, compared to Mercury, one day on Venus is just slightly longer than one year. Along with Mercury, Venus does not have any natural moons. Unique to Venus, it actually rotates opposite of every other planet! On the surface, you would see the sun rise in the West and set in the East.
